Thanks The Boring Flutter Development Show

A day like any other spent watching The Boring Flutter Development Show. Suddenly they begin to implement a beautiful watch, with a fantastic effect. The seconds, minutes and hours pass as if it were a calendar that is leafed through. I was amazed and amazed. I had to start from there to make my next application.

I already had a clock set up to show the time, but I don’t think a clock app is very useful to users. I thought of creating a countdown (or counter forward) to keep track of your events and above all to receive notifications when they expire.

The graphics were the hardest thing to fix but working hard and with a lot of passion in the end an extraordinary app came out, I think the most beautiful graphically created by me.
