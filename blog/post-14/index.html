I don’t want to live to work

I don’t want a life that forces me to work, I believe that our existence is the only opportunity we have to enjoy the time and we must necessarily enjoy it.

You think you are not forced to work, you really do it because you like it or it is the company that requires you to do it by telling you phrases like “if you do what you like you will not work one day of your life”. If I really weren’t forced to work I would rather spend my time with my family and the people I care about.

it’s all about money, if I had more money I certainly wouldn’t work, I’d pursue my interests, and I’d volunteer, it makes me feel good to help others.

I have always envied great managers and great entrepreneurs, not so much for their wealth but for their FREEDOM.

Being impressively rich allows you to give more value to your time, not to worry about spending 8 hours a day to bring home the salary every month.

What can I do to allow myself to fully enjoy my life as the rich managaer? How much do I miss to earn like them? But do you know how much they earn your one year salary? Most of the time it is about seconds.

Let’s translate my outburst into an application.
