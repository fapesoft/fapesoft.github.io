It all starts here

A normal day, a normal guy, these are the ingredients that pushed me to start. If you start you are already halfway through the work I thought, then why not try it?

I bought 2 courses on udemy, and started watching tutorials on tutorials on how to make a game with unity. I study computer science at the university but the study and the creation of games are two very different things, the university gives you the basics, you just have to put the desire and the passion into it ;). Books and tutorials, the ingredients are available for free on the internet.

After 2 months I made my first game, simple Invaders From Space. If I did it, you can certainly do it too, in programming the important thing is to start and jump into the code!
