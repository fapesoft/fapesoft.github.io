Still with this cinema

After more than 10 years my favorite saga is over: Avenger. You know when you leave the cinema with adrenaline in your body and think you can do everything? That’s how I felt when I left the cinema.

I want to translate my emotions into an application. I am inevitable!

I want to crumble the code into pieces and make something special, something that vanishes like Thanos made the Avengers vanish. What if I made the apps vanish?

Simple, an uninstaller where when the apps are uninstalled the effect of the movie is played, a click and the apps are uninstalled.
