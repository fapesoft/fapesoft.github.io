Replica

I’m still excited about the launch of my previous Animal sound app. I think it can be really useful to someone.

Every day I check its position in the store, I hope it goes up to be more visible to users.

I saw that in the store, in the apps that reproduce sounds, the ones that are more fashionable are those of the birds. On the other hand, the most beautiful and well-known songs in nature are those of birds.

What would you have done at this point? Simple I took the basis of the animal sound app, I worked on it a little, I updated graphic resources and images and I created an app dedicated to the sound of birds, expanding the catalog of birds present, always with the option to play guess their verse.
