Request Account Delete

From this form you can request the deletion of your Account and all data associated from the Apps that allow you to create an account.

Your data is saved on the servers in the form of email and password, only if you have created an account. Email is saved only at login, subsequent operations, such as sending questions, are saved with a reference id, so subsequent operations after login will not affect your email.

You can request the deletion of your account and data by filling out this form or directly in the app, after login. This operation will remove your account and all associated data, you can always create another account in the future with the same email.
