Flashlight

The fastest free flashlight.Open your flashlight in a flash with this app!

Free

The flashlight is a free, powerful and very fast app, it has been designed to open in a short time, in a flash, so as to allow you to have the bright light of your flashlight ready immediately when you need it, when the light is low. Eye-catching graphics show the status of the flashlight on the phone, a digital light turns on when you turn on the camera and off when you turn it off.

You can customize your lantern app in various ways, you can set different themes, you can decide the speed of the torch, whether to turn on the flashlight as sos, whether to leave the light on at the app exit or turn on the flash automatically when you open the application.

Everything is free, illuminate the dark with this app.
