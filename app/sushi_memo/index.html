Sushi memo

Create sushi orders online with your friends at the table, save your sushi order

Free

Ordering sushi together with your friends at the table is easy with sushi memo.

You can mark your friends' sushi orders in 2 modes.

Online: Everyone orders by choosing the dishes from the menu on his own phone, Sushi Memo will take care of combining the orders and showing you your total orders.

Offline: Choose the orders from the menu that you like best, you can use this mode to record your orders or record the total orders of the table in a single phone.

Sushi memo will save the executed orders: every time you go to an already visited sushi restaurant, you can easily reorder and review your past orders.
