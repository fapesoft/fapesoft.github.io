Yes or no

Answer the questions with yes or no, easy right? Yes or no?

Free

Yes or no it’s a simple fun board game where you have to answer yes or no to a series of questions that are asked, easy to play, right?

This game contains hundreds of questions and new questions are added every day. You can also add your yes or no questions, and follow their progress, see how many people vote yes and how many vote no.

Try this game with your friends, the answers will amaze you, not everyone will think the same way.

This game is suitable for children.

Playing is simple: just answer yes or no to the questions that appear on the screen (dare to answer, you cannot skip the choice). Think carefully about what to choose because some choices really put you in trouble. After answering yes or no, the buttons will be dynamically expanded, showing you clearly what other players have voted for and the total number of votes collected for that question.

Each trivia question is taken from the database based on the language of your mobile phone, only if this is not present is presented in English.
