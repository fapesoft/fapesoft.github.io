Shark vs Penguin

The shark is coming, runs penguin

Free

Hey penguin, don’t let the shark eat you and dodge the crates!

You are a penguin that runs over sheets of ice that appear beneath you at irregular intervals. You will have to try not to be fooled by the ravines in the middle of the slabs, so as not to fall prey to the shark, does not wait for you to make this fatal mistake.

To complicate your life, in addition to the slabs, there will be some boxes that will appear preventing you from proceeding further. In this case you will have to make a precise jump to cross them and proceed further.

The shark, eager to eat you, will try to jump towards you, faster and faster.

As you accumulate points the game becomes more difficult, in particular more boxes appear and the speed of the plates under your feet increases.

Will you get first place in the standings?
